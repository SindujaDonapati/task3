Disney princesses

Cinderella

Cinderella is a kind-hearted young woman, forced to endure a cruel stepmother and stepsisters, who finds happiness at a royal ball , ultimately marrying the Prince after losing a glass slipper.

Rapunzel

Rapunzel is a young princess, imprisoned in a tower by a witch, known for her long, magical golden hair which allows her to be accessed by others, fueling her desire to explore the world beyond her confinement.

Belle

Belle is imaginative and smart, and comfortable being authentically herself. She adores a good book, because different stories bring her to new places,and allow her to see new perspectives.

Ariel

Ariel's sense of adventure is infectious, encouraging those around her to see the world differently. This trait makes her not only a strong-willed character but also a relatable one for both children and adults.

Snow White

Snow White is a kind, beautiful princess forced to flee from her jealous stepmother, the Evil Queen, finding refuge with seven dwarfs in the forest, where she is ultimately saved by true love's kiss.

Jasmine

Jasmine is Strong-willed and defiant extremely compassionate and caring towards the people in her kingdom, her family, and her friends, especially her most loyal friend, her tiger Rajah.
